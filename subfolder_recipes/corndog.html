Corn Dogs

Description

A corn dog is a sausage/hot dog that is been skewered, coated in a cornmeal batter, and deep-fried

The street food was invented by German immigrants in Texas in the 1920s

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Beef frankfurters

Steps

  1. Make the batter by combining the dry ingredients, then stirring in the milk and egg
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and roll in the batter
  3. Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly browned
